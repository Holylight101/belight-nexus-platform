# BeLight Nexus AI — Business Model & Monetization Plan
## A World-Class AI Workforce Platform for Modern Businesses

---

## Vision

BeLight Nexus AI is the world's most intelligent AI workforce platform. We don't just automate tasks — we think, analyze, and strategize like a senior consultant, legal advisor, and compliance officer combined. Our Autopilot Agent orchestrates multiple specialized AI agents to deliver unified, actionable business intelligence that no single tool can match.

---

## Revenue Model: SaaS + Usage-Based Hybrid

### Tier 1: Starter (Free Forever)
- **Price:** $0
- **Target:** Solo founders, early-stage startups, students, nonprofits
- **Includes:**
  - 10 Autopilot runs per month
  - 5 contract analyses per month
  - 3 grant searches per month
  - Basic compliance checklists
  - Email support (48h response)
- **Goal:** Viral adoption, word-of-mouth, network effects

### Tier 2: Professional ($49/month)
- **Price:** $49/month or $490/year (17% discount)
- **Target:** Small businesses, growing startups, freelance consultants
- **Includes:**
  - Unlimited Autopilot runs
  - 50 contract analyses per month
  - 25 grant searches per month
  - Full compliance monitoring with alerts
  - Export to PDF / Markdown
  - Priority email support (24h response)
  - 2 team members
- **Goal:** Primary revenue driver for 1-50 employee businesses

### Tier 3: Business ($199/month)
- **Price:** $199/month or $1,990/year (17% discount)
- **Target:** Mid-market companies, law firms, consulting agencies, NGOs
- **Includes:**
  - Everything in Professional
  - Unlimited everything
  - Custom agent training (upload your own compliance docs, contract templates, grant databases)
  - API access (rate limit: 10,000 requests/month)
  - White-label dashboard (your logo, your colors)
  - Dedicated account manager
  - 10 team members
- **Goal:** Enterprise pipeline, high LTV

### Tier 4: Enterprise (Custom)
- **Price:** $999+/month (annual contract)
- **Target:** Large enterprises, government agencies, international NGOs, law firms with 100+ lawyers
- **Includes:**
  - Everything in Business
  - Unlimited API access
  - On-premise deployment option
  - Custom agent development (build new agents for your use case)
  - SLA: 99.9% uptime
  - 24/7 phone support
  - SSO / SAML integration
  - Unlimited team members
- **Goal:** 6-figure annual contracts, strategic partnerships

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Hackathon + Community (Months 1-3)
- Win Track 4 of the Autopilot Agent hackathon
- Open-source the core framework (AgentOS) to drive developer adoption
- Build community on Discord, LinkedIn, Twitter/X
- Target: 10,000 free users

### Phase 2: Product-Market Fit (Months 4-6)
- Launch paid tiers
- Partner with startup accelerators (Y Combinator, Techstars, 500 Startups) as preferred vendor

- Geographic expansion: EU, UK, Australia, Canada
- Target: $2M ARR

### Phase 4: Platform (Year 2)
- BeLight Marketplace: third-party agents built by developers
- BeLightOS: operating system for AI-powered businesses
- IPO preparation or strategic acquisition by Microsoft, Salesforce, or Anthropic
