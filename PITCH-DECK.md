## SLIDE 3: The Solution

**BeLight Nexus AI — The World's First Multi-Agent Business Intelligence Platform**

Instead of one generic AI, we built 4 specialized agents that work together:

1. **RegulaBot** — Compliance monitoring & action checklists
2. **ContractSense** — Contract risk analysis & negotiation tips
3. **GrantRadar** — Grant discovery & application drafting
4. **Autopilot** — Orchestrates all 3, delivering unified strategic briefings

**The magic:** Autopilot classifies your intent, calls the right agents in parallel, and synthesizes their outputs into one actionable plan.

## SLIDE 5: Technical Architecture

```
User Query → Autopilot
                ↓
    Intent Classification (Qwen LLM)
                ↓
    ┌──────────┴──────────┐
    ↓                     ↓
RegulaBot            GrantRadar
(Compliance)        (Funding)
    ↓                     ↓
    └──────────┬──────────┘
               ↓
    Synthesis Engine (Qwen Max)
               ↓
    Unified Strategic Briefing
```

**Stack:** FastAPI + Next.js + Qwen (DashScope) + Supabase + Alibaba Cloud ECS

**What makes it unique:** Parallel agent execution with intent classification and synthesis. No other tool does this.
